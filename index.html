Record Guide

You're not making a commercial. You're recording yourself doing the work. That IS the content.

Screen Record Flow (your daily work = content)
1
Open QuickTime
File > New Screen Recording
Or: Cmd+Shift+5
2
Narrate as you go
Talk through what you're doing and why. Don't script it. Just explain like you're showing Gabby.
3
Do the actual work
Build a tool. Prep for Women Build Safety. Write curriculum. Use a tool you made. That's the content.
4
Stop + Upload
Save. Drop in Google Drive > RAW FROM JOYI. Tell Gabby in Discord.
iPhone Selfie Flow (the personal hook)
1
Face a window
Natural light on your face. Phone at eye level.
2
Say the hook
One sentence. Look at the lens. "I quit my career a year ago."
3
Tell the story (60s)
The TPC arc. Why this matters to you personally. No script needed — just talk.
4
Upload to Drive
Share from Photos app directly to Google Drive > RAW FROM JOYI folder.
